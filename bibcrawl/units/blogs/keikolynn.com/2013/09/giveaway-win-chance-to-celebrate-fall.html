Giveaway: Win a Chance to Celebrate Fall Fashion with Me at T.J.Maxx!

I know I just proclaimed my newly discovered love for summer, but I'm already looking forward to dressing for fall. So much so that I’ll be joining Brooklyn Blonde, My Style Pill and Be Chic Mag to co-host an exclusive fall fashion event at T.J.Maxx! I headed into T.J.Maxx recently to take a look at all of the fall items they have to offer, and picked up a few key pieces for the start it the season. I love to layer, and the majority of my wardrobe is geared toward that notion. In my eyes, everything is more interesting with varied textures, colors, and proportions - and layering gives you so many options for styling pieces in completely different ways. What I'm looking forward to the most is sweater weather: give me an oversized sweater and I will wear it until it unravels. I combined my love of layering and large sweaters for this casual ensemble from T.J.Maxx, balancing the loose-fitting, chunky knit with a button up blouse and slim fitting jeans. A pair of high heels, bright swing bag, and bold red lips dress it up just a bit, but a pair of streamlined flats or oxfords would be another great option for extra comfort.


Want to start building your fall wardrobe and find some of the best designer trends? You can win attendance to an in-store shopping event at T.J.Maxx on September 12th with my friends and me - and you'll get a $100 gift card to get you started, as well as transportation to and from the event! 
  To enter, share the fall trend you are most excited about by commenting below on this post - or you can enter through my twitter, facebook, or instagram by tagging @keikolynn and @tjmaxx with the hashtag #MaxxStyleScout. 
I will be choosing five winners, which will be announced on Tuesday, September 10th. 
 Note: You can submit your entries on multiple social media channels for additional opportunities to win (that means you have up to four entries). To be eligible to win, readers must live within a 2 hour radius of Manhattan and be available on the morning of Thursday, September 12th for the exclusive, in-store event.
